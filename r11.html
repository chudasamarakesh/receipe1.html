RICE


Rice is a rich source of carbohydrates, which are the body's primary source of energy. It provides readily available energy for daily activities and physical exercise.

Rice is naturally low in fat, particularly saturated fat, making it a heart-healthy choice when prepared without excessive amounts of added fats.

Unseasoned rice is naturally low in sodium, which is beneficial for those watching their sodium intake, especially individuals with hypertension or heart conditions.
