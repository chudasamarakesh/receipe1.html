PAVBHAJI


you can customize the recipe to make it healthier by reducing the amount of butter or ghee used for cooking and choosing whole wheat rolls instead of white bread.

Some versions of Pav Bhaji include legumes like peas or beans, which add protein to the dish.

The combination of carbohydrates, vegetables, and spices in Pav Bhaji can be quite satisfying, helping to control appetite.
