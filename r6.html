DHOSA


Dosa batter is typically made from a combination of rice and urad dal, providing a balanced mix of carbohydrates and proteins.

The fermentation process enhances the bioavailability of nutrients and promotes the growth of beneficial probiotic bacteria, aiding digestion and gut health.

Dosas are naturally gluten-free, making them suitable for those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease
