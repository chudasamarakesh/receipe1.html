PIZZA


Cheese is a significant component of pizza, and it provides a good amount of calcium, which is essential for bone health.

If you choose whole wheat or whole grain crust, pizza can be a source of dietary fiber, which aids in digestion and helps maintain stable blood sugar levels.

ingredients like tomatoes and bell peppers are rich in antioxidants, which can help protect your cells from damage caused by free radicals.
