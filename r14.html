PASTA


Pasta is a carbohydrate-rich food, providing a significant source of energy for the body. Carbohydrates are the primary source of fuel for your muscles and brain.

Pasta is naturally low in fat, particularly saturated fat, which is beneficial for heart health when prepared with lean sauces or toppings.

Pasta contains complex carbohydrates, which are digested slowly, leading to a steady release of energy and helping to keep blood sugar levels stable.
