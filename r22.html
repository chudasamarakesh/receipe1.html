UTTAPAM


Uttapam can provide various essential vitamins and minerals, including B vitamins (such as folate and niacin), iron, magnesium, and potassium.

The fermentation process involved in making uttapam batter introduces beneficial probiotic bacteria, which can be good for gut health and digestion.

ttapam is typically prepared with minimal added fats, making it a relatively low-fat dish, especially when compared to fried foods.
