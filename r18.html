DALBATTI


Dal Baati provides a good amount of plant-based protein from the lentils used in the dal. Protein is essential for muscle repair, immune function, and overall health.

Dal Baati can provide various essential vitamins and minerals, including B vitamins (such as folate and niacin), iron, magnesium, and potassium.

When prepared traditionally, Dal Baati is relatively low in fat, especially saturated fat, making it a heart-healthy option.
