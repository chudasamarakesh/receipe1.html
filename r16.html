DAHI PURI


Dahi Puri contains yogurt (dahi), which is a good source of protein. Protein is essential for muscle repair and overall health.

Yogurt contains probiotics, which are beneficial bacteria that support gut health and digestion. Consuming probiotics can help maintain a healthy balance of gut flora.

Depending on the ingredients used for the filling and toppings, Dahi Puri can provide vitamins and minerals like vitamin C, vitamin A, folate, and potassium.
