IDLI


idli is typically low in fat, especially when compared to fried snacks. It is usually steamed, not fried, which keeps the fat content low.

The combination of rice and urad dal in idli batter makes it a complete source of protein. Protein is essential for muscle growth, repair, and overall health.

: Idli is a source of dietary fiber, especially if made with brown rice or whole-grain rice. Fiber aids in digestion, supports regular bowel movements, and helps control appetite.
