DABELI


Dabeli is primarily made from white bread buns and potato filling. Both of these ingredients are rich in carbohydrates, which can provide energy. However, excessive consumption of refined carbohydrates may lead to weight gain and blood sugar spikes.

The potato filling in dabeli is made from boiled and mashed potatoes, which provide some dietary fiber, vitamins (like vitamin C and B vitamins), and minerals (such as potassium). However, the use of deep-fried potatoes can increase the calorie and fat content of the dish.

Dabeli is known for its flavorful and spicy taste due to the use of various spices like red chili powder, cumin, and chaat masala. Some of these spices may have digestive benefits and can add flavor without adding too many calories.
