ALOO CHIPS


Potatoes, the main ingredient in aloo chips, are a good source of potassium, which is essential for heart health and maintaining proper muscle and nerve function.

Some aloo chips may retain a portion of the potato's vitamin C content, which is an antioxidant that supports immune function and skin health.

Aloo chips are enjoyed in various cultures and hold cultural significance in many regions.
